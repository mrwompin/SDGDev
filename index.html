Welcome!

The Software Development Guild is a gathering of student software developers who seek to further advance software craft through collaboration outside of the classroom. The Guild serves as a meeting place for students of all backgrounds and understanding who seek to better understand software components and the development cycle.

The Guild meets on Tusedays during the Spring and Fall semesters, meetings are from 4pm to 5pm. To join the Guild just come to a meeting! Don't let busy schedules stop you, if you would like to join but can't make it to the meetings contact the project manager at MR/MRS_Manager@dudeguy.com to see what you can do to help on our next project!

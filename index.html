The Guild

The Software Development Guild is a gathering of student software developers who seek to further advance their craft through collaboration outside of the classroom. The Guild serves as a meeting place for students of all backgrounds and understanding who seek to better understand software components and the development cycle.

Meetings are Tuesday from 4:00pm to 5:00pm.

if you would like to join but can't make it to the meetings contact the project manager at MR/MRS_Manager@dudeguy.com to see what you can do to help on our next project!
